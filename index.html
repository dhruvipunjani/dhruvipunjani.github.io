01 About

I'm a final-year BBA (Hons) Finance student with a deep fascination for understanding what makes businesses truly valuable. While most see financial statements as numbers, I see them as stories waiting to be uncovered stories about competitive advantages, management quality, and long term potential.

My approach to investing is rooted in value investing principles, inspired by the philosophies of Warren Buffett and Charlie Munger. I don't just ask what a company does, I dig deeper to understand why it succeeds, how it creates value, and where it might be headed. Whether it's dissecting annual reports late into the night or building financial models for case competitions, I'm driven by genuine curiosity about how businesses work.

Through academic projects including equity research reports, stock pitch competitions, and financial modeling assignments, I've developed a rigorous analytical framework. I'm currently pursuing CFA Level 2, constantly sharpening my skills to transition from student to analyst. I'm seeking opportunities where I can contribute fresh perspectives and grow alongside experienced investment professionals.
